How to Slice Watermelon

Ever wanted something refreshing on a nice warm day? Follow this recipe to learn how to make it happen!

Ingredients and Tools:

  1. Watermelon
  2. Long, sharp kitchen knife

Steps:

  1. Cut both ends off of the watermelon.
  2. Balancing the watermelon on one end, slice directly down the middle.
  3. Place the watermelon down on the side you just cut, then slice down the length.
  4. Slice each section into 2-inch wide slices, width-wise.

You are now done! Wasn't that easy?

Watermelon Ice Cream

Ingredients and Tools:

  1. Watermelon
  2. Milk
  3. Sugar
  4. Vanilla Extract

Steps:

  1. Blend the watermelon (without rind), 2 cups milk, 2 tbsp sugar, and 1.4 tsp. vanilla.
  2. Pour into a freezer-friendly container such as a pan or plastic bucket.
  3. Place in the freezer and remove after an hour. Remove cream from pan and re-blend. Repeat this process 2 times.

Now enjoy that ice cream!

Watermelon Caprese Salad

A refreshing salad for a hot summers day!

Ingredients and Tools:

  1. Watermelon
  2. Chopped Strawberries
  3. Feta

Steps:

  1. Combine watermelon, strawberries, feta, and olive oil. Season according to taste
